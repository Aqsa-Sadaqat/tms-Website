Activities in Kashmir

Details about activities in Kashmir.

  1. Shikara Ride on Dal Lake

    2. Glide along the tranquil waters of Dal Lake in a traditional wooden shikara, marveling at the stunning views of the surrounding mountains, lush gardens, and floating markets. This iconic experience allows you to soak in the serene atmosphere and witness everyday life on the lake.

  2. Houseboat Stay

    3. Immerse yourself in the unique charm of Kashmir by staying in a luxurious houseboat on Dal Lake or Nigeen Lake. These intricately crafted floating homes offer a blend of comfort and tradition, with ornate woodwork, cozy interiors, and panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

  3. Trekking in the Himalayas

    4. Embark on an exhilarating trekking adventure amidst the majestic peaks of the Himalayas. Kashmir offers a variety of trekking routes catering to all skill levels, from gentle hikes through picturesque valleys to challenging expeditions to high-altitude mountain passes.

Activities in Swat

Details about activities in Swat.

  1. Malam Jabba

    2. Malam Jabba is a renowned ski resort and one of Pakistan's premier destinations for winter sports. Nestled in the Hindu Kush mountain range, it offers facilities for skiing, snowboarding, and other snow-related activities.

  2. Kalam Valley

    3. Kalam Valley, situated at an altitude of about 6,800 feet, is a major tourist attraction known for its stunning natural beauty. The valley is surrounded by snow-capped mountains, dense forests, and beautiful lakes.

  3. Saidu Sharif

    4. Saidu Sharif, the administrative capital of Swat, is rich in cultural heritage and historical sites. It houses the Swat Museum, which showcases a collection of Gandhara art, ancient artifacts, and Buddhist sculptures.
